Metropolis (1927)

Digital, 148 mins, Silent

Rating:

G

Language(s):

German

Credits:

  • Fritz Lang as Director
  • Thea Von Harbou as Writer

Synopsis:

A son of the corporate elite frees enslaved workers in a futuristic city-scape in this Weimar classic that laid the bedrock for modern sci-fi.

Personal Comments:

This is earliest, most influential film in the sci-fi genre. Metropolis is a living and breathing place, with its own culture and unique aesthetic that blends the gothic with the modern and the industrial. You have played videogames and watched movies with a futuristic city-scape. Here is a film older than your grandparents and you will see those city-scapes in the vistas of Metropolis.
