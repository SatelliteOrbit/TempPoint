Midnight Run (1988)

Digital, 126 mins

Rating:

PG

Language(s):

English

Credits:

  • Robert DeNiro as Jack Walsh
  • Charles Grodin as Jonathan "The Duke" Mardukas
  • Yaphet Kotto as Special Agent Alonzo Mosely
  • John Ashton as Marvin Dorfler
  • Joe Pantoliano as Eddie Moscone

Synopsis:

A gruff bounty hunter must take an accountant on the bad side of the mob from New York to Los Angeles as he outruns the police and the mafia.

Personal Comments:

It isn't a bias I've cultivated on purpose, but some of my personal favourites are buddy-cop roadtrip movies. This film is not as well-known as it deserves to be. The chemistry between DeNiro and Grodin elevates this movie from a worthwhile watch into an emotional rollercoaster. A cross-country roadtrip between two conflicting characters who open up to each other is just too good to pass up.
