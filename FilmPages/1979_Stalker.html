G

  • Nikolai Grinko as the Professor
    • Voiced by Sergei Yakovlev

  • Alisa Freindlich as the Stalker's Wife

Synopsis:

A Professor and a Writer are guided through the mysterious and dangerous Zone by the Stalker.

Personal Comments:

My first exposure to Tarkovsky. This film is very slow and deliberate. That's not to say it's boring, the long pans of the camera show strange landscapes, beautiful imagery, and expressive emotion on the actors' faces. The movie is more abstract and experimental. It is a mysterious and slow-burning examination of faith and the human capability for innocence in the modern age.
