Synopsis:

Two brother sheep farmers overcome their years-long feud through a time of trial and tribulation.

Personal Comments:

An emotionally gripping story about family rivalry accompanied by beautiful vistas of rural Iceland. It looks as good as it's written, recommend watching on as large a screen as possible.
