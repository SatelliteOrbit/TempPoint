Nightcrawler (2014)

Digital, 118 mins

Rating:

PG+

Language(s):

English

Credits:

  • Jake Gyllenhaal as Lou Bloom

Synopsis:

A sociopathic petty thief records violent events in the city by night and selling the footage by day.

Personal Comments:

A well-written and well-acted character study from the "literally me" film genre. On top of Lou Bloom being an interesting character to watch, the film also deals with themes relating to mass media consumers' desensitization towards violence. Consider watching Drive if you liked this one, or watching this if you liked Drive.
