Synopsis:

A stunt driver by day and getaway driver by night develops a relationship with his neighbour and her child as they all become entangled in an organized crime plot gone wrong.

Personal Comments:

Literally me. The best feature of the movie is the silence, that seconds-long space in between characters' dialogue. By saying nothing between each other, tension is heightened and characters feel more human. That seems like a weird thing to say but you'll understand when you see it. It's a very good watch, one of my favourites.
