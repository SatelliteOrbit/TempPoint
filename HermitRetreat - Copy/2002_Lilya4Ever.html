Synopsis:

A young girl is abandoned by her mother and must fend for herself on the cruel streets of post-Soviet Russia.

Personal Comments:

Probably one of the darker and more depressing movies in my collection. It is well shot and well-acted. Everything that occurs feels grounded and gut-wrenching. Just when you think it can't get worse, it does. I should say the movie has graphic depictions of sexual violence.
