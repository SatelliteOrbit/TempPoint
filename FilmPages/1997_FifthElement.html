Synopsis:

An alien works with a retired special forces major to avert a great cosmic evil from destroying humanity.

Personal Comments:

There are two types of people. There are those who love the Fifth Element and there are those who despise it. I have yet to meet anyone in between. Though I can't blame someone for hating this movie, I love it anyway. I love the action, I love the sets, I love the costumes and the settings, I love everything. The presentation and artistic detail more than make up for a plot that is otherwise formulaic and predictable. No matter how many times I watch it it always keeps me hooked. A worthy entry into the annals of sci-fi film.
