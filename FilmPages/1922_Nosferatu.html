Synopsis:

A mysterious count unleashes a horrible evil upon a small German village.

Personal Comments:

This movie is a must-watch if you're looking into silent film at all. Considering the artistic direction, you'll be left with some images you can never unsee.

Though it is roughly based on the Bram Stoker novel Dracula, the filmmakers never actually got the rights. In a lawsuit filed by Stoker's estate, it was decided that all copies of the film be destroyed. Thankfully, it wasn't. This fate was unfortunately a rarity among silent films (an estimated 80-90% of silent films are now considered lost).
