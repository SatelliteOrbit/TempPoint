Synopsis:

Two lost foreigners travelling on business to Tokyo develop an unlikley relationship.

Personal Comments:

A visually-stunning romance. The neon-lit urban setting gives this movie a distinct look and feel. The film explores the breakdown and grinding of relationships using the foreign setting. The sense of alienation and loneliness felt by the characters in a foreign and unfamiliar culture is a way of externalising their inner sense of isolation. A saddening and intensely emotional film.
