Digital, 95 mins

Rating:

PG

Language(s):

English

Credits:

  • Drew Goddard as Director and Writer
  • Joss Whedon as Writer
  • Chris Hemsworth as Curt Vaughan

Synopsis:

What may look like just another slasher film is not as it seems.

Personal Comments:

I have amazing memories watching this one with my friends years-back. It's a fun twist on the archetypal slasher movie that adds comedy and retains horror and violence. Highly recommend if you're into things like the SCP Foundation, Control (the videogame), or the X-Files.
