Synopsis:

A dark, violent, comedic social critique and horror classic where an upper-class yuppie investment banker slowly descends into a murderous reverie.

Personal Comments:

Although at times funny, this film still retains a darkness and an edgy quality. Christian Bale carries this movie from start to end and his character is elevated by the witty dialogue and a mind-melting plot driven by an unreliable narrator. At times pulpy, funny, and dark. A worthy watch and a great character study.
