Credits:

Synopsis:

An oil magnate moves his operation into a small town and descends into greed and isolated misanthropy as he feuds with a Christian preacher and faith healer.

Personal Comments:

This movie is mainly focused around a single character. As the film goes on, you begin to question the initial assumptions you made about him. An amazing examination of greed and the American Dream.
