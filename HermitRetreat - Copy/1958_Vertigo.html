Rating:

Credits:

Synopsis:

When a recently retired police detective is tasked by a friend to investigate his mentally unstable wife, he finds that everything is not as it seems.

Personal Comments:

Look at those credits, James Stewart (yes, again) and Alfred Hitchcock. Full of romance and suspense, the mysteries in this film have a slow burn and high payoff. You'll be on the edge of your seat for most of it and standing up by the end.
