Synopsis:

A disgraced journalist is hired by a rich man to solve the 40-year-old disappearance of his daughter.

Personal Comments:

This movie is the rare case where an American remake surpasses the original. This film is just a well-done mystery where characters must navigate through drama associated with decades-long family feuds. Be warned, there are graphic depictions of sexual violence.
