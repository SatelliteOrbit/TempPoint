World of Tomorrow (2015)

Digital, 16 mins

Rating:

G

Language(s):

English

Credits:

  • Don Hertzfeldt as Director, Writer, and Animator
  • Julia Pott as Future Emily
  • Winona Mae as Present Emily

Synopsis:

A little girl communicates with her future self.

Personal Comments:

Don't let the runtime fool you, this film is full of heart. The voice acting is superb, combined with the animation and narrative it'll leave you in the afterglow of melancholy by the end. I will definitely be looking into Hertzfeldt's other work after this.
