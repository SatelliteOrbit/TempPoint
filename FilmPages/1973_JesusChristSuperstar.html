Synopsis:

A film based on a passion play of the same name examining the last few days of the life of Jesus Christ.

Personal Comments:

One of the best musicals out there. The story is told entirely through song, there is no spoken dialogue. The songs are very well-written and the movie has a distinctive visual style.
