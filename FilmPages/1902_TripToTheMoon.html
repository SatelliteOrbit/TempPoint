A Trip To The Moon (1902)

Digital, 15 mins, Silent

Rating:

G

Credits:

  • Georges Méliès as Writer and Director

Synopsis:

A group of scientists go to the Moon.

Personal Comments:

A silent classic from the age where film was a novelty. This movie has so much heart poured into it and it's amazing to see these pioneering actors and filmmakers see how much they can do with this medium. From what I hear there's a colourized version of this out there made by Star Film. Not colourized as in shot on colour film, colourized as in a bunch of technicians painted the frames by hand. Amazing.
