Digital, 102 mins

Rating:

PG

Language(s):

English

Credits:

  • Logan Lerman as Charlie Kelmeckis
  • Emma Watson as Samantha Button
  • Ezra Miller as Patrick Stewart
  • Mae Whitman as Mary Elizabeth

Synopsis:

A socially withdrawn teenager goes through his turbulent freshman year of highschool in this romance and coming-of-age drama.

Personal Comments:

This movie oozes with hipster aesthetics and imagery. I didn't anticipate seeing cassette tapes being actively used in a movie set in the 2010's. This is a very famous, household name in the romance genre, and for good reason. The story is fun and compelling and all the emotional moments hit their mark.
