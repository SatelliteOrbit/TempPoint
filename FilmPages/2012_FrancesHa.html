Frances Ha (2012)

Digital, 86 mins

Rating:

PG

Language(s):

English

Credits:

  • Noah Baumbach as Director and Writer
  • Greta Gerwig as Frances Halladay and Writer

Synopsis:

A dancer living in New York carves out a life for herself.

Personal Comments:

This movie attracts film majors the way a bug zapper attracts moths and flies. I'm not sure if it was necessary to make the whole thing in black-and-white but goddamn does it make the whole movie look good. It takes some balls to think you can write, and star in a movie and Greta Gerwig pulls it off 100%. Highly recommend, it's a very cozy watch.
