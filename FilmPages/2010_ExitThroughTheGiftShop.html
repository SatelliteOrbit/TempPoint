Exit Through The Gift Shop (2010)

DVD, 87 mins

Rating:

G

Language(s):

English

Credits:

  • Banksy as Director

Synopsis:

A documentary examining the rise of street art and its associated subculture transitions into a an interesting narrative about the cameraman himself.

Personal Comments:

An amazing and thought-provoking documentary. The historical aspect of the film that examines street art is informative, but following along with these artists and seeing them do their work is intensely compelling. A personal favourite.
