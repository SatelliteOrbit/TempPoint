Synopsis:

A Los Angeles-based gang of robbers are pursued by a ruthless homicide detective.

Personal Comments:

An amazingly well-done movie. Great performances give life to interesting and compelling characters. On top of just being a good action movie, the romantic and family drama in between the action setpieces is strong in its own right.
