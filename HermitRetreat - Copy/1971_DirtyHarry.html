Synopsis:

A rugged cop must roll up his sleeves and take the law into his own hands in order to stop a sadistic rampaging murderer.

Personal Comments:

An exciting and action-packed pursuit of a savage villian by a rugged but admirable hero against the backdrop of a dark, cruel setting. This film is certainly more fun than it is inquisitve, but Harry Callahan is a compelling and fun-to-watch character who cements this movie as a classic.
