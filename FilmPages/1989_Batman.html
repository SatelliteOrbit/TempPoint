Personal Comments:

Tim Burton's artistic direction is combined with film noir and '80s camp. This movie definitely has style and a unique look. The action is great and the story is at once gritty and comedic. This is the movie that brought Batman to the mainstream. If you like the Dark Knight series, you have this film to thank.
