Crank (2006)

Digital, 87 mins

Rating:

X

Language(s):

English

Credits:

  • Neveldine and Taylor as Directors and Writers
  • Jason Statham as Chev Chelios

Synopsis:

A hitman seeks revenge after being injected with a poison that will kill him if his adrenaline drops too low as he rampages through Los Angeles.

Personal Comments:

The closest thing we'll ever get to a good videogame movie. That's not to disparage the movie, it is incredible to watch. Violent, sexual, edgy, and full of unrepentant action. It's not a deep examination of the human condition, but it's a great piece of cinema I can recommend nonetheless.
