Synopsis:

A man who cannot create new memories must track down and kill his wife's rapist/murderer.

Personal Comments:

Christopher Nolan's breakout hit. If you like some good action with a mind-bending premise you'll like this movie. Interesting, three-dimensional characters combined with events cloaked in mystery and ambiguity makes this movie worth watching and rewatching.
