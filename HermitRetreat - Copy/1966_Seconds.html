Seconds (1966)

Digital, 107 mins

Rating:

PG+

Language(s):

English

Credits:

  • Rock Hudson as Antiochus Wilson
  • John Randolph as Arthur Hamilton

Synopsis:

An average middle-aged man has the opportunity to be reborn and fulfill all his wants and desires, only to discover the hellish consequences.

Personal Comments:

The deeper you go into this movie, the more the horror of the premise begins to be understood. It certainly should have been cut down by a couple of scenes where the plot just meanders around, but it is a worthy watch. It is gut-wrenching and terrifying.
