Synopsis:

A mentally ill World War II veteran is roped in by an eccentric but charismatic figure.

Personal Comments:

A movie that is loosely adapted from the rise of Scientology and the life of L. Ron Hubbard. It examines cults, who joins them, and why they do it. Joaqin Phoenix plays a unique, eccentric, and interesting character (what's new?) and is accompanied by some very impressive credits. This movie is more sexual than I thought it would be. A bit of a melancholy ending but a very solid movie overall.
