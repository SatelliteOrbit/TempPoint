Synopsis:

A Spanish veteran of noble blood dawns a black cape to defend his poor town against its greedy government and corrupt military officials.

Personal Comments:

This is not the first movie to feature Zorro, but it is the first Zorro talkie. A swashbuckling adventure where a lone hero fights the power and gets the girl. The film rests entirely on the charisma and acting prowess of Tyrone Power and he succeeds 100%. If you like superhero movies, you'll like this.
