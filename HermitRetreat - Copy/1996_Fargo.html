DVD, 98 mins

Rating:

PG+

Language(s):

English

Credits:

  • Joel and Ethan Coen as Writers and Directors
  • Frances McDormand as Marge Gunderson
  • William Macy as Jerry Lundegaard
  • Steve Buscemi as Carl Showalter
  • Peter Stormare as Gaear Grimsrud

Synopsis:

The plan of a used car salesman to con his father-in-law by hiring two thugs to kidnap his wife goes wrong.

Personal Comments:

A well-done crime thriller that incorporates violence and grit with the Coen's signature black humour. The story is exciting and the acting is great across the board. A personal favourite. There was also a TV show made based on this movie where each season is different story within the same universe. From the first 3 seasons I've watched it seems very good.
