Synopsis:

A family of superheroes must fight against an evil organization while reconciling their own familial conflicts.

Personal Comments:

Released the year I was born. Funnily enough it did have quite a place in my childhood. The animation and action alone is well-done and the family dynamic gives the movie a solid emotional core. There is nothing I can say about it that hasn't already been said, go watch it.
