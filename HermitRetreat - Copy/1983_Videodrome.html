Videodrome (1983)

DVD, 89 mins

Rating:

PG+

Language(s):

English

Credits:

  • David Cronenberg as Writer and Director
  • James Wood as Max Renn
  • Debbie Harry as Nicki Brand

Synopsis:

A TV executive is seduced and psychologically reprogrammed by a mysterious TV channel.

Personal Comments:

A graphic and unnerving commentary on mass media and violence. It is well-done from a technical perspective but I'm not sure how I feel about the quality of the plot. My favourite part about this movie is the dreary and unnerving atmosphere it creates visually and emotionally.
