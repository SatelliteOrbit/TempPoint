Credits:

Synopsis:

A movie examining the seasonal and cyclical nature of romance.

Personal Comments:

One of my personal favourite romance movies. The film uses non-linear story-telling to show how a subjective and biased point-of-view changes the way we view the past and how we perceive the breaks in our relationships.
