Dr. Strangelove (1964)

Digital, 94 mins

Rating:

PG

Language(s):

English

Credits:

  • Stanley Kubrick as Director and Writer
  • George C. Scott as General Buck Turgidson
  • Peter Sellers as Group Captain Lionel Mandrake, President Merkin Muffley, and Dr. Strangelove
  • Sterling Hayden as Brigadier General Jack D. Ripper

Synopsis:

A rogue U.S. Air Force general orders a nuclear attack on the Soviet Union and the American heads of military and state must scramble to avert the apocalypse in this comedic Cold War thriller.

Personal Comments:

Leave it to Kubrick to make a comedy about the nuclear holocaust. Somehow, the comedic nature of some scenes (and the entire premise) does not detract from the dire consequences. The jokes hit just as hard as the possibility of human extinction. One of my personal favourites and one you should see.
