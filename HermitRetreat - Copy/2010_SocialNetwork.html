The Social Network (2010)

Digital, 120 mins

Rating:

PG

Language(s):

English

Credits:

  • David Fincher as Director
  • Jesse Eisenberg as Mark Zuckerberg
  • Andrew Garfield as Eduardo Saverin
  • Justin Timberlake as Sean Parker

Synopsis:

A college student changes the world by creating a new way for people to connect over the internet and must face the consequences.

Personal Comments:

This movie is a journey where an infinitely ambitious character destroys himself and others on his rise to the top. It examines how the basic model of social interaction is shifted by the internet. All of this is acted out by top-of-the-line actors and accompanied by an amazing score by Trent Reznor and Atticus Ross.
