Scott Pilgrim vs. the World (2010)

DVD, 113 mins

Rating:

PG

Language(s):

English

Credits:

  • Edgar Wright as Writer and Director
  • Michael Cera as Scott Pilgrim
  • Mary Elizabeth Winstead as Ramona Flowers

Synopsis:

To be with the girl of his dreams, Scott Pilgrim must fight her Seven Evil Exes.

Personal Comments:

A heavily stylistic and fun romantic comedy. Not only is the setting visually-unique, but Wright packs a bunch of little details into the background that keep rewatches interesting. The acting is great and the characters are well-written, funny, and compelling.
