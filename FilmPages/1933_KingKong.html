Synopsis:

An adventerous documentarian's plan to capture the mythical King Kong on film goes wrong when he and his crew must rescue the head actress from dinosaurs, savages, and the giant ape himself in the jungle island forgotten by time.

Personal Comments:

When you think of movies that pioneered special effects, you probably think of Avatar, Toy Story, Titanic, Jurassic Park, or any number of Marvel movies. You probably don't think of a black-and-white film from 1933 where a big monkey steals a woman. The stop-motion animations and special effects were revolutionary and laid the groundwork for blockbusters to come.
