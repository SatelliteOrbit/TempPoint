Nameless Gangster (2012)

Digital, 133 mins

Rating:

PG

Language(s):

Korean

Credits:

  • Choi Min-Sik as Choi Ik-Hyun
  • Ha Jung-Woo as Choi Hyung-Bae
  • Cho Jin-Woong as Kim Pan-Ho
  • Kwak Do-Won as Jo Beom-Seok

Synopsis:

An unremarkable but well-connected customs officer enters the world of organized crime against the backdrop of an '80s Korea ruled by corruption.

Personal Comments:

An amazingly well-done twist on the typical gangster, "rags-to-riches" movies you're familiar with. If you love crime movies this one will be a very pleasant addition to your library.
