Minority Report (2002)

Digital, 145 mins

Rating:

PG+

Language(s):

English

Credits:

  • Steven Spielberg as Director
  • Tom Cruise as Chief John Anderton

Synopsis:

In a future where murders are predicted before they happen, a police detective is convicted of a future crime and must prove his innocence while on the run.

Personal Comments:

Not a lot of people associate this movie with Spielberg. The marketing focused more on Tom Cruise as a result of 2001's A.I. (written and directed by Spielberg) performing poorly in the States. The action is well-done but what really steals the show in this movie is the setting. I have not seen a depiction of a low-life cyberpunk future quite like this one. I'm sure the premise has some plot holes or breaks with logic, but I can't pinpoint any and I'm sure they're minor. Highly recommend.
