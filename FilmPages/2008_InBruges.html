Rating:

PG+

Language(s):

English

Credits:

Synopsis:

A pair of contract killers are tasked to hide out in the Belgian town of Bruges.

Personal Comments:

Thoughtful writing seamlessly mixes comedic and tragic elements while conveying themes relating to dreams, death, morality, faith, and the afterlife. One of my personal favourites.
