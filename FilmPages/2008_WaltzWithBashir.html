Synopsis:

A veteran of the 1982 Israeli-Lebanon War reconstructs his lost memories of his time in the war.

Personal Comments:

Another entry into the "war is hell" genre. The animation creates fantastical and creative images that are intertwined with an examination of the darkness and violence present in the human soul. It's a highly recommended war movie.
