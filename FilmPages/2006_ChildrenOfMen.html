Synopsis:

18 years after the last human child was born, a cynical and dejected office worker accompanies a refugee on a dangerous journey through a dystopian Britain on the brink of collapse.

Personal Comments:

This movie alongside Minority Report has my favourite depiction of the near future. On top of great artistic direction, long takes of deeply intricate scenes make the world feel alive and real. One of my favourites.

This movie is based on a novel of the same name by P.D. James. The book itself is very good and worth reading. The film adaptation differs quite a bit from the source material, regardless I love both equally.
