Synopsis:

A group of teenage camp counselors at an outdoors summer camp in the forest rumored to be cursed are killed off one-by-one.

Personal Comments:

I like this movie as a time capsule. The effects are very well-done but I wouldn't describe this movie as frightening. It's still nice to watch for its use of violence and gore effects.
