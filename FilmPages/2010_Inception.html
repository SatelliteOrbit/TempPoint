Rating:

G

English

Credits:

  • Christopher Nolan as Writer and Director

  • Elliot Page as Ariadne
  • Tom Hardy as Eames

Synopsis:

A group of extractors infiltrate dreams to carry out corporate espionage.

Personal Comments:

It's a great action movie that carries insights into dreams. A very interesting thematic throughline in this movie is how dreaming relates to filmmaking. Nolan's signature incorporation of practical effects whenever possible adds greatly to the action sequences. The plot is not only exciting but mysterious and intriguing, inviting debate and rewatches. Highly recommend.

The greatest flaw of this movie is that I have to watch it on a television. I will never know what it was like seeing this thing in the cinema either normally or on IMAX.
