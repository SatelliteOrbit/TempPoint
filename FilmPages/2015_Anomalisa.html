Synopsis:

A beautifully animated film that you should really go into blind.

Personal Comments:

On top of its masterful animation, this movie is a great examination of alienation, romance, and mental illness. The most interesting thing of this movie is how it has a deeply rooted aspect that is not immediately obvious. Each person who watches this will probably notice this deeply rooted aspect at different points in the film. Highly recommend.
